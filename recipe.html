PANEER CURRY

Ingredients

  1. Paneer
  2. Onions
  3. Tomatos
  4. Salt
  5. Oil
  6. Ginger garlic paste
  7. Turmeric
  8. Chilli powder
  9. Coriander powder
  10. spices
  11. Coriander leaves

Making Process

  • Marinate the paneer with the chilly powder,salt,coriander powder and spieces rest it for 5 minutes
  • After that take a pan and add some oil and fry the panner pieces until it turns to soft pieces
  • In the same oil fry the onions and tomatos until it chab=nges the colour
  • Grind the tomatos and oninons in soft paste
  • In a pan add some oil and spices after add the smooth paste cook it for 5 mins and add the ginger garlic paste, turmeric, chilly powder and coriander powder
  • And cook it of 2 mins and add the paneer pieces and some water cook it for 5 mins
  • Afer 5 mins add coriander leaves and turn off the flame
  • Finally the tastiest paneer curry is ready and enjoy the meal☺️.
